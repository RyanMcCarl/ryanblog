Farewell, Shaman Drum

30 Jun 2009 art, retail, business, books

Shaman Drum of Ann Arbor, MI, one of the best independent bookstores in the Midwest, permanently shut its doors today. It is a major loss for Ann Arbor and for book-lovers everywhere. Independent and used bookstores need your support. Shop at them. Go to their events. Buy their gift certificates as holiday gifts. Independent bookstores must fight back against the challenging economic environment by doing everything they can to improve their businesses.

Becoming a teacher and a Michigan Wolverine

29 May 2009 University of Michigan, education, teaching, cross-country, books, Chicago, writing, personal

I’m excited to report that I have decided to enter the University of Michigan School of Education’s Secondary MAC (MA in Education with Secondary Certification) program in mid-June. The program is 12 months long and includes over 1,000 hours of classroom experience as a student teacher and substitute teacher, resulting in full certification. I look forward to finding unique ways to show my students the importance of learning and reading as well as the value of informed engagement with current events and politics.
