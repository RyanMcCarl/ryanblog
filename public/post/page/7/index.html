R.I.P., Richard John Neuhaus

8 Jan 2009 Judaism, Richard John Neuhaus, journalism, public intellectual, Christianity, religion

A great American public intellectual has died: Richard John Neuhaus, the founding editor of First Things. Neuhaus was a liberal Lutheran minister active in the Civil Rights and antiwar movements of the 1960s who converted to Roman Catholicism and became a staunch, vocal social conservative. I disagreed with him about most things, but I have the highest respect for his talents as a writer, and First Things is the best conservative magazine available on the intersection of religion and culture.
