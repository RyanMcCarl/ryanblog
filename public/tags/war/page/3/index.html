Mortimer J. Adler on war and peace

“Our view of war, then, must be broadened to include both armed conflict and battles of diplomacy, economic aid, and propaganda. War is war, whether it is “hot” or “cold.” The struggle for power and prestige among the nations goes on all the time. Only the means vary, and whether these be armed force or diplomatic pressure or other nonviolent means depends on the occasion. It follows, then, that peace is not merely a negative thing - the absence of armed conflict.
